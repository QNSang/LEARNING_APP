# Product Vision

## Ten huong san pham

**AI Learning OS** hoac **KnowledgeMap Learning System**.

Day la ung dung hoc tap dung AI de bien tai lieu thanh ban do tri thuc, sau do dung ban do do de lap lo trinh hoc, tao bai kiem tra, theo doi muc do thanh thao va ca nhan hoa cach hoc.

## Van de can giai quyet

Nguoi hoc thuong gap 5 van de:

1. Tai lieu dai, kho biet nen hoc phan nao truoc.
2. Doc xong nhung khong biet minh da hieu that hay chua.
3. Kien thuc bi roi rac, khong thay moi lien he giua cac khai niem.
4. Khong co lich on tap khoa hoc nen nhanh quen.
5. Chatbot AI tra loi duoc, nhung khong tao thanh qua trinh hoc co cau truc.

## Gia tri cot loi

App nen tap trung vao 4 gia tri:

- **Understand**: giai thich kien thuc dua tren tai lieu goc.
- **Map**: bieu dien kien thuc thanh graph co quan he.
- **Practice**: tao cau hoi, flashcard, bai tap va cham diem.
- **Adapt**: dieu chinh lo trinh hoc theo muc do thanh thao cua nguoi hoc.

## Dinh vi san pham

Khong nen dinh vi la:

```text
Upload PDF -> chatbot hoi dap
```

Nen dinh vi la:

```text
Upload learning material -> AI builds a personal learning map -> user learns through guided practice
```

## User chinh

- Sinh vien can hoc tu giao trinh, slide, paper.
- Nguoi tu hoc cong nghe, AI, lap trinh, kinh doanh.
- Giao vien muon bien tai lieu thanh bai hoc, quiz va learning path.
- Nguoi on thi can he thong hoa kien thuc.

## Nguyen tac san pham

1. Moi cau tra loi AI phai co nguon tu tai lieu.
2. Moi node kien thuc phai co vai tro hoc tap ro rang.
3. Graph khong chi de nhin dep, ma phai dieu khien learning path.
4. App phai khuyen khich active recall, khong chi doc thu dong.
5. Tinh nang AI phai co cache, retry va fallback vi API co the loi/quota.
